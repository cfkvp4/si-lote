How to Play Powerball

How to Play

  1. Purchase a Ticket: Each ticket costs a base amount of 0.0006 ETH.
  2. Choose Your Numbers:
    • Select 5 unique numbers from 1 to 69.
    • Pick an additional Powerball number from 1 to 26.
    • If preferred, use the "Generate Random Numbers" option for automatic selection.
  3. Save Your Ticket and Wallet Details: Ensure you keep your ticket and have access to the wallet used for the purchase.
  4. Drawing Schedule: Drawings occur on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays at 10:59 PM ET. Numbers drawn include:
    • 5 white balls (numbers from 1 to 69).
    • 1 red Powerball (number from 1 to 26).

    Winning Rules:

    • Match the Powerball to win.
    • Match two or more white balls, with or without the Powerball, for smaller prizes.
    • Match all five white balls and the Powerball to win the jackpot.

Claim Your Prize

  • Winning tickets must be reported to the contact email provided by the organization.
  • Validation and verification occur only upon request by participants claiming a winning ticket.
  • Once verified, the corresponding prize will be awarded.

Restricted Ticket Sale Hours

  • Sales Close: 10:30 PM EST / 3:30 AM UTC (before the drawing).
  • Sales Reopen: 1:00 AM EST / 6:00 AM UTC (after the drawing).
  • Important: Tickets purchased during restricted hours are not valid for any drawing and will be disqualified.

How Timing Works

  1. Sales Close Before the Drawing: Ticket sales close about an hour before the drawing (10:30 PM ET or 3:30 AM UTC).
  2. After the Drawing: Sales resume but tickets are valid only for the next scheduled drawing.
  3. Example:
    • If you buy a ticket on Wednesday at 8:00 PM ET, it participates in the same night's drawing at 10:59 PM ET.
    • If you buy a ticket on Thursday at 12:00 AM ET, it participates in the next drawing on Saturday at 10:59 PM ET.

Using Blockchain for Ticket Registration

If using blockchain to record tickets, the "Input Data" field in the transaction ensures transparency and immutability.
